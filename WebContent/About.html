About

 

The LNMIIT Grading Portal is a software module that automates the process of maintaing, modifying and publishing the academic records of the students of LNMIIT, Jaipur. It supports three kinds of users:
  • Instructors
  • Students
  • Admin


Important Notes:
  • The following File formats are supported for updating the database through the provided GUI: *.csv
  • The following format is to be followed while uploading the database modifying file:


    • For Instructor: | RollNumber | CourseId | Quiz1 | Quiz2 | Quiz3 | Quiz4 | MidSemMarks | EndSemMarks | Total |                            Grade |

    For Admin: | CourseId | CourseName | Credit | FacultyId | Instructor Name |
